The Kind of Light that Shines on Texas

Nailor and Mrs. Wickham helped Oakley to his feet. His already fattened nose bled and swelled so that I had to look away. He looked around, bemused, walleyed, maybe scared. It was apparent he had no idea how bad he was hurt. He didn’t blink. He didn’t even touch his nose. He didn’t look like he knew much of anything. He looked at me, looked me dead in the eye, in fact, but didn’t seem to recognize me.
